Hotels in Belém

Some recommendations for hotels in Belém

Ibis Styles Belém Batista Campos

Set 3 km from bustling Ver-o-peso market, this straightforward hotel is 2 km both from the 17th-century Forte do Presépio and from the Emílio Goeldi Museum.

Address: R. Pres. Pernambuco, 116 - Batista Campos.
Contact number: (91) 3342-2121

Mercure Belém Boulevard

Set in a modern high-rise in the city's central shopping district, this upscale hotel is an 8-minute walk from the Museu Cultural - UFPA and a 12-minute walk from the ornate Basílica Nossa Senhora de Nazaré.

Address: Tv. Dom Romualdo de Seixas, 1560 - Umarizal.
Contact number: (91) 3366-7575

Google Maps Link

Bristol Umarizal Belém Hotel

In a building with a glass facade, this casual hotel is 2 km from the Guajará Bay coast, 3 km from the Ver-o-peso market and 7 km from the Belém–Val-de-Cans–Júlio Cezar Ribeiro International Airport.

Address: Tv. Dom Pedro I, 1104 - Umarizal.
Contact number: (91) 3349-6326

Radisson Hotel Maiorana Belém

This modern, glass-fronted hotel is set 1.6 km from the Museu Paraense Emílio Goeldi research institution and 10 km from Val de Cans International Airport.

Address: Av. Cmte. Brás de Aguiar, 301-321 - Nazaré.
Contact number: (91) 3182-8797

Hotel Princesa Louçã

Offering views of Guajará Bay, this laid-back hotel set among shops and restaurants is a 16-minute walk from Ver-o-Peso, an open-air market, and 9 km from Aeroporto Internacional de Belém.

Address: Av. Pres. Vargas, 882 - Campina.
